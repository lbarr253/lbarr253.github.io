             When it comes to my design philosophy, I design the emotion behind the idea. For example, the collectionn shown with the blue and green dresses was inspired by the musical Wicked. I drew from the idea that life is not what it seems and the dark and lonely feelings to create the dark tones and imposing dresses that almost seem like armor. 
                In designing, I lean toward classic shapes, prefering to draw out the wearer than call attention to what's being worn. I also embrace the femininity that god has given us and use it to create a quiet but powerful look with each garment that I design.

                   My current career goal is to be a personal designer and stylist so I can both personalize what I create to my client and make masterpiece, one-of-a-kind gowns that are elaborate and complexe.
